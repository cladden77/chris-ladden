ShareArizona

OVERVIEW

Steve with ShareArizona engaged my services for the creation of a comprehensive brand identity and the development of a dynamic website for his new real estate venture, ShareArizona, headquartered in Northern Arizona. ShareArizona serves as a pivotal platform connecting property sellers with potential buyers, facilitating property listings and providing detailed specifications for available land and properties.

Code: https://github.com/cladden77/sharearizona

Stack: WordPress, HTML, CSS/SASS, PHP, Advanced Custom Fields

Sharing the Best of Arizona

Key Objectives

  1. Brand Identity: Crafting a distinctive and memorable brand identity that embodies ShareArizona’s mission and values while resonating with its target audience.
  2. Website Development: Designing and developing a user-friendly and visually appealing website that effectively showcases property listings and facilitates seamless interaction between sellers and buyers.
  3. Functionality: Ensuring the website integrates essential features for property listing submissions, search functionality, and detailed property specifications to enhance user experience and engagement.

Deliverables

  1. Logo Design: Creation of a unique and recognizable logo that reflects ShareArizona’s identity and distinguishes it within the real estate market.
  2. Website Design and Development: Development of a fully functional website with intuitive navigation, responsive design, and robust backend infrastructure to support the dynamic nature of property listings and user interactions.
  3. Content Management System (CMS): Implementation of a user-friendly CMS for easy management and updating of property listings and website content.
  4. Search Engine Optimization (SEO): Optimization of the website for improved search engine visibility and organic traffic generation.
  5. Training and Support: Provision of training and ongoing support to Steve for managing and maintaining the website effectively.

Outcome

The completed brand creation and web design/development package for ShareArizona equip Steve with a compelling brand identity and a powerful online platform to streamline property transactions and foster connections between sellers and buyers in Northern Arizona’s real estate market.
