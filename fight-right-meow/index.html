Fight Right Meow!

OVERVIEW

Fight Right Meow, the innovative creators of a captivating card game, entrusted me with the design and development of their e-commerce website. With a mission to bring joy and excitement to players worldwide, Fight Right Meow aimed to establish a robust online platform to showcase and sell their unique card game product.

Live Site: https://fightrightmeow.com

Stack: WordPress, HTML, CSS/SASS, PHP, Advanced Custom Fields, Woocommerce

An Olathe-based Tax Prep and Bookkeeping company

Key Objectives

  1. Product Showcase: Designing an engaging and visually appealing e-commerce website that effectively showcases the Fight Right Meow card game, highlighting its features, gameplay, and unique selling points.
  2. User Experience Optimization: Creating an intuitive and seamless user experience throughout the website, from product browsing and selection to checkout and payment, to maximize conversion rates and customer satisfaction.
  3. E-commerce Functionality: Implementing essential e-commerce functionalities, including product catalog management, secure payment processing, order management, and customer support features, to facilitate smooth and efficient transactions.
  4. Mobile Responsiveness: Ensuring the website is fully responsive and optimized for mobile devices, allowing users to browse and purchase the Fight Right Meow card game seamlessly across various screen sizes and devices.

Deliverables

  1. Custom Website Design: Developing a custom website design tailored to Fight Right Meow’s brand identity and product aesthetics, incorporating captivating visuals and engaging content to captivate visitors.
  2. E-commerce Development: Building a robust e-commerce platform using a scalable and secure content management system (CMS), allowing for easy management of products, orders, and customer data.
  3. Payment Gateway Integration: Integrating a secure payment gateway through WooCommerce to facilitate safe and convenient online transactions, offering multiple payment options to accommodate customer preferences.
  4. SEO Optimization: Conducting SEO optimization to improve the website’s visibility in search engine results, targeting relevant keywords and optimizing product pages for maximum exposure and organic traffic.

Outcome

The successful design and development of the Fight Right Meow e-commerce website have provided the company with a powerful online platform to showcase their unique card game product and drive sales. With its intuitive user experience, seamless e-commerce functionality, and captivating design, the website effectively engages customers and facilitates the purchase process, contributing to the continued success and growth of Fight Right Meow.
